Betting on “over 1.5 goals” means predicting that there will be at least two goals scored in a football match by both teams combined. To win an over 1.5 goals bet, the total number of goals scored in the match must be 2 or more. For example, if the final score is 2-0, 1-1, or any other combination that results in at least two goals, the bet wins. The over/under betting market is popular due to its simplicity and frequent opportunities for bettors to gauge game dynamics based on team form and defensive capabilities. Approximately 70-80% of football matches typically end with more than 1.5 goals scored, making it possible wins for bettor's.
