Both Teams to Score (BTTS) is a popular betting market in football where the bettor predicts whether both teams in a match will score at least one goal each during the game. The outcome of the match itself does not matter as long as both teams find the net, the bet wins.


Remember When placing a BTTS bet, you have two options:


Yes: You believe that both teams will score at least one goal.


No: You believe that at least one team will not score.


BTTS focuses solely on goal-scoring rather than match outcomes.t can add excitement to matches since you’re invested in both teams scoring.it allows for strategic betting based on team statistics and form.
